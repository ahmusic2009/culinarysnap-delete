Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Culinary Snap is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal data necessary to provide our service:

  • Recipe Data: When you submit a recipe URL, we process the page content to extract recipe information. This data is stored in your personal account.
  • Account Information: If you create an account, we store your email address and name for authentication purposes.
  • In-App Activity: We collect basic information about how you interact with the app (such as recipe imports, saves, and settings changes) to improve functionality and user experience.
  • Diagnostics & Crash Data: We collect platform-level diagnostic and crash data to ensure app stability, identify bugs, and improve performance.
  • Device Identifiers: We collect device or other identifiers used by our platform infrastructure for analytics, crash reporting, and security purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To extract and display recipe content from URLs you provide
  • To save your recipe collection for future access
  • To improve app functionality and fix bugs
  • To authenticate your account and secure our service

3. Data Storage & Security

Your recipe data is stored securely using encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) via our backend infrastructure provider. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

We use AI-powered services to parse recipe content from URLs. The URL content is processed to extract recipe data only and is not stored beyond what is necessary for the extraction.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Culinary Snap is a general-audience application. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

6. Your Rights

You may delete any saved recipe at any time. To request deletion of your account and all associated data, please use our Account Deletion Request page or contact us at support@ahmusicstudio.com.

7. Tracking & Advertising

Culinary Snap does not use any advertising SDKs, tracking pixels, or third-party advertising networks. We do not track you across other apps or websites. The in-app activity, diagnostics, crash data, and device identifiers we collect are used solely by us and our backend infrastructure provider to operate and improve the app — they are not shared with third parties for advertising.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@ahmusicstudio.com.
